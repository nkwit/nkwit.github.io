Diseases are everywhere

I am determined to stop them.

As an Epidemic Intelligence Service Officer with CDC's Division of Vector-Borne Diseases I have experience finding practical insights among large datasets and investigating public health problems of domestic and international significance. I am passionate about exploring human and animal connections in disease risk and timely dissemination of compelling information for public health action.
